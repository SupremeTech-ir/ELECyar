Introduction

The LPC1768FBD100 microcontroller has a 32-bit ARM Cortex M3 processor core made by NXP and operates at a frequency of 100MHz. This microcontroller provides 512KB of FLASH memory on the chip. The LPC1768FBD100 microcontroller has 70 input / output pins and communication protocols: I2C, SPI UART / USART.

The LPC1768FBD100 microcontroller has a working voltage of 3.3 volts and operates in the temperature range of -40 to +85 degrees Celsius.
