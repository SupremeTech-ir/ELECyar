اتوماسیون خانگی و صنعتی

The TIANBO HJR1-2CL-05V is a compact and highly sensitive telecom relay featuring a DPDT (2 Form C) contact configuration. Designed for applications requiring precision, reliability, and low coil power consumption, this relay operates with a nominal coil voltage of 5VDC and a coil power of just 0.36W, making it ideal for power-efficient systems. It comes in a DIP package suitable for through-hole PCB mounting and offers a sealed construction to enhance durability in harsh environments. The relay provides high insulation between coil and contacts, ensuring safe and stable operation, and is capable of switching up to 240VAC or 60VDC at a maximum current of 2A. It has fast response times (operate time: 8ms, release time: 4ms), robust mechanical endurance, and complies with UL, cUL, and TÜV safety standards. This makes it suitable for use in telecommunication devices, control systems, and other precision electronic circuits.
