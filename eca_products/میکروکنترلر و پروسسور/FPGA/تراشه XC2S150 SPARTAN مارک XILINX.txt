Introduction

The SPARTAN XC2S150 series FPGA chip has 150,000 logic gates and a commercial value of 200 MHz by XILINX, which is a suitable processor and high frequency applications. This chip offers 6.9 KB of RAM supplied on the chip. SPARTAN XC2S150 chip has 140 pins input / output, 864 LABs / CLBs and 3888 logic elements per cell

The SPARTAN XC2S150 chip has a working voltage of 25 volts and operates in the range of 0 to +85 degrees Celsius.
