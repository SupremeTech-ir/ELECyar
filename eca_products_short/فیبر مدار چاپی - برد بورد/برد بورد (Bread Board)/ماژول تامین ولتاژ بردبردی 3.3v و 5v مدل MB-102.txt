Introduction

The MB102 power module is a practical module for educational and development projects based on breadboard. This module is designed for direct mounting on the breadboard and is able to supply two separate channels with voltages of 3.3 V or 5 V using two AMS1117 voltage regulators. The voltage selection for each section is possible independently and via a jumper. This design makes the module very suitable for working with a wide range of modules, sensors and microcontrollers.

To provide input, a 6.5 to 12 V adapter can be used via the DC jack or 5 V power can be used via the micro USB port.
