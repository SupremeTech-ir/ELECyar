The STP80NF10 transistor is part of STMicroelectronics' STripFET family and is specifically designed for high-power, low-loss switching applications. The STripFET structure increases efficiency and reduces heat under heavy loads by reducing on-state resistance. With a standard voltage rating of 100V and current rating of 80A, this device is a good choice for designing DC-DC converters, UPS systems, industrial motor control, and switching power supplies. Fast switching timing and controlled input capacitance allow it to be used in circuits with rapid voltage and current changes. This MOSFET also offers optimal heat transfer, easy installation, and heat sink mounting with its TO-220 package, making it very useful for industrial and semi-industrial projects.

Total power dissipation (25 °C body temperature): 300 W
Continuous operating temperature: -55 °C to +175 °C
