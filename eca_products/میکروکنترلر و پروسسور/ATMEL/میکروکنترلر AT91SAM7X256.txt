Introduction

The AT91SAM7X256 microcontroller has an ARM7TDMI processor core made by ATMEL and operates at a frequency of 55 MHz. This microcontroller provides 256 KB of FLASH memory on the chip. AT91SAM7X256 microcontroller has 60 input / output pins and communication protocols: I2C, SPI UART / USART.

The AT91SAM7X256 microcontroller has a working voltage of 1.8 volts and operates in the temperature range of -40 to +85 degrees Celsius.separate battery supply, and up to 165 general purpose I/O pins.
