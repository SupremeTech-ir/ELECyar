Introduction

The AN6550 is a dual operational amplifier with a phase compensation circuit built-in, allowing low voltage operation.It is suitable for application to various electronic circuits such as active filters and audio preamplifiers.

Features

Phase compensation circuit
High voltage gain, low noise
Output short protection circuit
Low voltage operation(±2.5V to±10V)
