Introduction

The WS2811 IC is one of the most widely used digital LED drivers used in color lighting projects and addressable LED strips. This IC is part of the LED driver family and allows for precise control of all three colors, red, green, and blue, independently. The WS2811 has three PWM constant current outputs that provide up to 256 levels of brightness (8 bits) for each color.

This chip uses a serial data (DIN) to receive color information and passes the data to the next chip in the chain (DOUT) after processing. This chain structure allows for the control of a large number of LEDs independently and addressably.
