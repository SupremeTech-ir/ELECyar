This product is a high-power CREE LED with a moonlight color temperature (6500–6000K) assembled on a 20mm diameter aluminum star heatsink. This design helps to dissipate heat better and increase the useful life of the LED. The chip used has high light intensity with good energy efficiency. For proper operation, it requires a power supply between 3 and 3.6V DC with a current of about 700 to 850mA. This module does not have an internal current limiting resistor, so the use of a constant current driver is required.

Specifications

Operating voltage: 3 to 3.6 V DC
Power consumption: 3 W
Operating current: 700 to 850 mA
Color temperature: 6000 to 6500 K (moon white)
Chip type: CREE High Power (probably XP-G or XP-E2)
Base dimensions: 20 mm (star shape)
Base material: High heat transfer aluminum
Internal resistance: None (constant current driver required)
Installation: Screw or thermal adhesive to heat sink
