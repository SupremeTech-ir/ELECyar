Introduction

Our PolySwitch families of telecommunications and networking devices help meet the growing demand for resettable overcurrent protection. These product families help provide protection against damage caused by power cross and power induction surges as defined in ITU, Telcordia GR1089 and UL60950. Our offering includes chip, surface-mount and radial-leaded configurations.
