Introduction

The STM32F107RCT6 microcontroller has a 32-bit ARM Cortex M3 processor core made by STMicroelectronics and operates at a frequency of 72 MHz. This microcontroller provides 256KB of FLASH memory on the chip. The STM32F107RCT6 microcontroller has 51 input / output pins and communication protocols: I2C, SPI UART / USART.

The STM32F107RCT6 microcontroller has a working voltage of 2.4 to 3.6 volts and operates in the temperature range of -40 to +85 degrees Celsius.
