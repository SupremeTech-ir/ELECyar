نمونه-سازی-های الکترونیکی

This is a small-form-factor relay with a 9 V DC coil. When the coil is energized, the relay closes its SPST Normally-Open contact, allowing current to flow through the load circuit. It can switch loads up to 3 A at 125 VAC, making it ideal for controlling small electrical devices or signal-level switching circuits. The coil resistance is 255 Ω, with a coil power consumption of roughly 850 mW, ensuring low power draw and quick response. Its through-hole mounting and 5-pin configuration ease integration into PCB boards, and its small size makes it suitable for DIY projects, home automation modules, and light-duty control systems.
