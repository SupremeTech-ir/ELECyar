The HW-585 module is a fan speed controller based on PWM (Pulse Width Modulation) technology, designed for 12V DC fans up to a maximum current of 5A. By changing the pulse width, this module adjusts the fan rotation speed according to the ambient temperature or system needs, which reduces energy consumption, reduces noise and extends the life of the fans. The module can support 3 independent fans in two output channels. The first channel has a control range of 20% to 100% (or 40% to 100% if the TFL function is enabled) and the second and third channels have an adjustment range of 10% to 100%. The temperature sensor used is a 50k B3950 NTC type, which measures temperature changes with high accuracy and allows the module to react intelligently. This module also has an audio warning mechanism in case of fan failure or jamming; If a fan jam is detected, it first beeps and then turns the fan off and on again after the problem is resolved. The automatic and precise operation of this module makes it very suitable for cooling systems in electronic equipment, computer cases, servers and DIY projects.
