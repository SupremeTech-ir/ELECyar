Introduction

The STM8S003K3T6C microcontroller has an 8-bit STM8 processor core made by STMicroelectronics and operates at a frequency of 16 MHz. This microcontroller provides 8 KB of FLASH memory on the chip. The STM8S003K3T6C microcontroller has 28 input / output pins and communication protocols: I2C, SPI UART / USART.

The STM8S003K3T6C microcontroller has an operating voltage of 2.4 to 3.6 volts and operates in the temperature range of -40 to +85 degrees Celsius.
