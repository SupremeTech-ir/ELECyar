The choke or common mode filter transformer is an important component in filtering electromagnetic noise from the input and output lines of electronic boards. This transformer, with two coaxial coils, eliminates common mode noise and prevents the transmission of EMI/RFI interference to the circuit. This model with a UU10.5 core and a value of 10mH is suitable for use in switching power supplies, adapters, chargers and industrial equipment, and increases stability, reduces circuit temperature and prevents disruption to the operation of other devices.
