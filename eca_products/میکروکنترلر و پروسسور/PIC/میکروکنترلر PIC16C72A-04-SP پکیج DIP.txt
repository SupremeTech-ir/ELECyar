Introduction

The PIC16C72A-04/SP microcontroller has an 8-bit PIC16 processor core made by Microchip and operates at a frequency of 4 MHz. This microcontroller provides 2 KB of FLASH memory on the chip. The PIC16C72A-04/SP microcontroller has 22 input / output pins and communication protocols: I2C, SPI UART / USART.

The PIC16C72A-04/SP microcontroller has an operating voltage of 4 to 5.5 volts and operates in the temperature range of 0 to 70 ° C.
